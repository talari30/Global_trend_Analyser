This graph presents the evolution of a specific country's contribution to the global medical workforce over a series of years. It quantifies the country's share of medical doctors and dentists as a percentage of the total number in the world. The horizontal axis (X-axis) represents the timeline across various years, while the vertical axis (Y-axis) denotes the percentage contribution of the country's medical personnel relative to the global total. The graph is expected to show fluctuations that reflect the country's investments in healthcare and medical personnel distribution.
