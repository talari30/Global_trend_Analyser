Project Details

The Global Trend Analyzer is an application designed to provide insightful trend analyses by amalgamating various data dimensions. It focuses on presenting global trends through an intuitive GUI, offering users the ability to customize their queries by selecting specific countries, continents, or regions and time frames of interest. The application is equipped with complex queries that analyze and visualize data across different indicators such as GDP, education levels, happiness scores, and health metrics. Its reactive interface allows for real-time adjustments and interpretations of data, catering to the needs of researchers, policymakers, and anyone interested in understanding global developments and their historical context.
